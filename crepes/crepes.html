Crepes

Description

A delicious snack, easy to make and quick to bake.

It requires only a few very basic ingredients and the right flick of wrist.

Get ready to delight everyone!

Ingredients

    Flour 300g
    Milk 600ml
    Eggs 3
    Butter or oil (not olive!) to grease the pan

(You can add some flavour to the batter with vanilla extract or rhum for instance)

Steps

    Pour the flour in a bowl and make a hole in the middle
    Add the eggs in the hole
    Start to incorporate the eggs to the flour with a kitchen whip
    Add milk little by little as the mix starts to thicken
    Add your flavour of choice
    Heat a pan and grease it slightly, either with a bit of butter or using a tissue dipped in oil
    Add a ladle of batter, trying to cover the whole surface of the pan
    Flip the crepe over once the corners start to look crusty
    Let it cook a bit and put the crepe aside
    Repeat until you're out of batter
    Enjoy!
