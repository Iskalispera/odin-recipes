Lentil-bolognese

Description

This lentil Bolognese sauce is a delicious vegetarian option for pasta.

Rich in fibers and proteins, it's extremely tasty and just as nice as Bolognese with meat.

Ingredients

    1 onion, finely chopped
    1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
    1 carrot, diced
    10 mushrooms, thinly sliced
    2 cloves garlic, crushed and finely chopped
    200ml red wine
    200ml vegetable broth
    400g diced tomatoes
    400g lentils
    Olive oil
    Dried herbs (basil, paprika, oregano, rosemary, thyme...)

Steps

    Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saut√© until soft, about 5 minutes
    Stir in bell pepper and carrot; cook for 4 to 5 minutes. Add mushrooms; cook and stir until softened, about 2 minutes
    Pour in wine and simmer until slightly reduced, about 1 minute. Stir in diced tomatoes and vegetable broth.
    Bring sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until flavors combine, 10 to 15 minutes.
    Stir in lentils, paprika, basil, oregano, and herbs of your choosing
    Cook until lentils are heated through, about 5 minutes.
    Serve with the pasta of your choosing
    Enjoy!
